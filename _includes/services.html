This archive is an initiative of the Urban and Community Forestry program at UConn Extension, and was supported by the Strategic Vision Implementation Committee of the University of Connecticut's (UConn) College of Agriculture, Health and Natural Resources, and UConn Hartford's Research on Resilient Cities, Racism and Equity.

Inspiration

The archive is named after Leah Thomas' "The Intersectional Environmentalist: How to Dismantle Systems of Oppression to Protect People + Planet".
