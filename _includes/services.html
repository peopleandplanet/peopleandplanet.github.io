The project's name was inspired by Leah Thomas' "The Intersectional Environmentalist: How to Dismantle Systems of Oppression to Protect People + Planet". We hope that as with Thomas' book, this archive can be used as a tool that "promotes awareness of the fundamental truth that we cannot save the planet without uplifting the voices of its people".
