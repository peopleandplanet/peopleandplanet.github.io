About the archive

The people and planet archive is a growing list of digital stories that combine text and multimedia to define key concepts relating to nature, people and justice.

This is an educational repository informed by educators at the University of Connecticut (UConn). The archive can be used for virtual or face-to-face learning in both formal and informal educational environments, and by anyone and anybody who wants to learn about just environmentalism.

To use the archive, hover over the images below and click on key concepts to learn their definitions and history through digital stories.
