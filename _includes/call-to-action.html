About the project

The People and Planet project is a series of events and resources that provide empowerement and healing to people via natural spaces. The project is tailored with local input, paying close attention to the needs of those who have been forcefully silenced through history.

The People and Planet project is both a dedicated space to connect people with local experts who can provide support in issues that exarcebate social inequity as well as a place to empower people, especially women of color, by celebrating their resiliency and providing healing through nature.
